Battle Pushups

Turn every push-up into a move in the game. Battle Pushups runs locally on an iPhone with a front-facing camera and iOS 17 or later.

Contact

Email rishabhmaini5@gmail.com. Include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Camera access

If camera access was denied, open iPhone Settings, select Battle Pushups, and enable Camera. Return to the app and start a new set.

Positioning

  • Place the iPhone where the front camera can see your full body.
  • Use a well-lit room and avoid strong backlighting.
  • Hold the top push-up position until the countdown begins.

Privacy and local data

Camera frames are processed on the iPhone and are never saved or transmitted. Workout history stays on the device. Delete individual sets from History, or delete the app to remove all remaining local data.

Policy summary

Battle Pushups does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or use personal data for advertising or tracking. The app has no accounts, analytics, advertising, backend, purchases, or third-party data services.

Device permissions for Personal Data access

Battle Pushups requests access to the front camera so it can detect push-up movement and control the game. Camera frames and pose information are processed temporarily on the device without saving or recording. They are never transmitted to the Owner or any third party.

Camera permission must be granted before camera information can be accessed and can be revoked at any time in iPhone Settings. Revoking permission prevents camera-based workouts from functioning.

Local workout data

Completed sets, rep counts, workout duration, dates, outcomes, and the last selected goal are stored only on the device. Users can delete individual sets from History. Deleting Battle Pushups removes all remaining app data from the device. The Owner cannot access or restore this local data.

Retention and sharing

Camera information is not retained. Local workout data remains on the device until the user deletes a set or removes the app. Battle Pushups does not disclose app data to third parties and does not transfer app data outside the device.

Children

Battle Pushups does not knowingly collect personal data from children or any other users.

User rights and requests

Because the Owner does not receive or store user data, there is no server-side account or personal-data record to access, correct, export, or erase. Privacy questions can be sent to the Owner using the contact details above. Users in the EEA may also lodge a complaint with their competent data-protection authority.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when Battle Pushups changes. The current policy will remain available inside the app and at this URL.
